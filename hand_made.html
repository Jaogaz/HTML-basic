HAND MADE BOOKS

My talents are limited to only design and web work, I am also very good with my hands. These books are from a book making class. I wanted to make some sketch books for my own personal use. I created a collection of them using bookboard, cloth, glue, and string coated with wax. My idea was to make the books look worn and tathered so that they have some character. I was very proud of how these came out. You can check them out below.
